Commands

!help

Display all commands and descriptions

!hacks

Cheat that you can use on an Anarchy server.

!server

Shows how many people are active on the server.

!invite

Send bot invite link.

!loop

Toggle music loop.

!lyrics

Get lyrics for the current playing song.

!move

Move songs around in the queue.

!np

Show now playing song.

!pause

Pause the current playing music.

!ping

Show the bot's average ping.

!play

Plays audio from YouTube or Soundcloud.

!pruning

Toggle pruning of bot messages

!queue

Shows all the music queue and now playing.

!remove

Remove song from the queue.

!resume

Resume currently palying music.

!search

Search and select videos to play.

!shuffle

Shuffle queue.

!skip

Skip the current song.

!skipto

Skip to the selected queue number.

!stop

Stops the music.

!uptime

Check the uptime.

!volume

**Premium Feature** Change the volume of current playing song.
